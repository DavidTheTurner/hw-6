\question{(B1.2)}{
    Let $\mathbf{GL}^+(n,\reals)$ be the subgroup
    of $\mathbf{GL}(n,\reals)$ consisting of all
    matrices $A$ such that $\det(A) > 0$.
    Consider the function
    $\mapdef{h}{\mathbf{GL}^{+}(n+1)}{\mathbf{S}(n+1)}$, given by
    \[
        h(A) = \transpos{A}JA - J,
    \]
    where $\mathbf{S}(n+1)$ denotes the space of $(n+1)\times (n+1)$ symmetric matrices.
    Prove that
    \[
        dh_A(H) =  \transpos{A}JH +  \transpos{H}JA
    \]
    for any matrix $H\in \mathrm{M}_{n+1}(\reals)$.
    \\\medskip
    Prove that $dh_A$ is surjective for all $A\in \mathbf{SO}(n, 1)$.
    \\\medskip
    {\bf Remark: }
    Parts (1) and (2) can be used to prove that $\mathbf{SO}(n, 1)$ is a smooth manifold of dimension $\frac{n(n+1)}{2}$.
}
