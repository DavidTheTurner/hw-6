Let $J$ be the $(n+1)\times (n+1)$ diagonal matrix
\[
    J = \amsmata{I_n}{0}{0}{-1}.
\]
We denote by $\mathbf{SO}(n, 1)$ the group of real $(n+1)\times (n+1)$ matrices
\[
    \mathbf{SO}(n, 1) = \{A\in \mathbf{GL}(n+1,\reals) \mid
    \transpos{A}JA = J\quad\hbox{and}\quad \det(A) = 1\}.
\]
